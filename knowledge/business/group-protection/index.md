# Group protection

## Protection policies covering your business, your employees and their loved ones

- Build a protection package that best suits your business with a range of life insurance, critical illness and income protection policies
- Flexible cover options to match the needs of your company and your budget
- Give your employees financial, practical and emotional support through a selection of wellbeing services

## Be there when your employees need you with group protection

With group protection policies, you can build a benefits package that could help you attract and retain the best of the best. Offering financial reassurance and support, these policies are a valuable addition to your employees’ benefit package. Plus, you'll get access to a range of added-value wellbeing services, which vary depending on the policy you choose.

Available to employers of all sizes, our Group Protection policies can be flexible to suit your needs and your budget, even down to different benefits for different groups of employees. You can have cover for as few as three employees or more.

## Introducing Aviva Flex-pertise™ for larger employers

If you’re looking for a flexible benefits package that adapts to work for both you as an employer and your employees, Aviva Flex-pertise™ could be just what you’re looking for.

It lets you choose a package that’s right for your business and your employees’ needs but also lets your employees choose the level of cover that suits them best.

### Your Aviva Flex-pertise™ package can include:

- Group Income Protection
- Group Life
- Spouse/Partner Life Assurance
- Group Critical Illness
- Spouse/Partner Critical Illness Cover\*
- Children’s Critical Illness cover (including child-specific conditions)\*

\*Only available if you or your employees choose Group Critical Illness.

Building a flexible protection benefits package in this way can help you attract and retain employees. You and your people will also get expert assistance when the need arises, including options that will help employees before they need to make a claim. And you can do it at a cost that suits you.

[Find out more about Aviva Flex-pertise™](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/aviva-flexpertise/)

## Flexible protection package to suit your needs

We've outlined some of the benefits each type of protection policy offers. You can pick one or more to add value to your benefits package.

### Group Life Insurance ![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/icons/business/user-icons/black-icons/shield-with-heart.svg)

- **Prompt payment**

In 2024, the average speed of payment across all group life claims was 2.96 days.[3](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/#gl-payment-speed)
- **Master Trust options**

Choose from Registered and Excepted Master Trust arrangements and let us take care of the legal responsibilities.
- **Focus on wellbeing**

As well as financial support, there's access to practical and emotional support through a range of wellbeing services.

[Explore Group Life Insurance "Go to group life page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/group-life-insurance/)

### Group Critical Illness ![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/icons/business/user-icons/black-icons/shield-with-plus.svg)

- **Child cover included** We automatically cover your employee’s children, providing help if they’re diagnosed with a specified critical illness, including child-specific conditions.
- **Spouse/partner cover available**

If you choose to offer Aviva Flex-pertise™, you employees can add critical illness cover for their spouse or partner.
- **Focus on wellbeing**

Access to a wide range of wellbeing services - including mental health support, Get Active discounts, and nursing support - plus the workplace training which can help you create a happier, healthier workforce.

[Explore Group Critical Illness "Go to group critical illness page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/group-critical-illness-insurance/)

### Group Income Protection ![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/icons/business/user-icons/black-icons/protection-briefcase.svg)

- **Early intervention and rehabilitation**

We can help you support your employees even before they need to take time out. In 2024, 86% of employees we provided with rehabilitation support returned to or remained at work.[4](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/#gl-payment-speed)
- **Expert case management support**

Our dedicated case managers work closely with your employees during the claims process, supporting them every step of the way.
- **Focus on wellbeing**

Access to a wide range of wellbeing services - including Aviva DigiCare+ Workplace, mental health support for employees and line managers, and cancer care support - plus the workplace training which can help you create a happier, healthier workforce.

[Explore Group Income Protection "Go to group income protection page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/group-income-protection-insurance/)

Wellbeing services are non-contractual benefits which Aviva can change or withdraw at any time. They're provided by Aviva and a handful of carefully selected third-party providers. Terms and conditions apply.
