## What is Aviva Flex-pertise™?

It's our flexible benefits solution for larger employers, giving employees more choice and control over their protection options. It offers a comprehensive range of Group Protection products, from life insurance to critical illness cover, so you can build a package that fits your business, and your employees can select the cover that suits their individual needs.

When we talk about Wellbeing services on this page, these are non-contractual benefits that Aviva may change or withdraw at any time. They’re provided by Aviva and a handful of carefully selected third-party providers. Terms and conditions apply.

## A comprehensive range of employee protection

Offering a well-rounded benefits package can help attract and retain top talent. With Aviva Flex-pertise™, you can choose cover with the flexibility to meet your employees’ needs and your budget. It gives your employees more choice and control over their benefits - while you stay in control of the options, coverage levels, and contribution that work best for your business.

### Group Critical Illness

If an employee is diagnosed with a critical illness or needs surgery defined by the policy, they’ll receive a lump sum payment. Child cover is included as standard.

- You decide the level of cover available and whether employees can increase it in steps of £10,000 or £25,000.
- We’ll make one lump sum payment if a claim is made.

[Learn more about Group Critical Illness "Open our group critical illness page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/group-critical-illness-insurance/)

### Spouse or Partner Critical Illness cover

Employees can add this through Aviva Flex-pertise™ if they already have Group Critical Illness cover.

- You choose whether this option is available.
- We’ll make a lump sum payment if a claim is made.

### Group Life Insurance

If an employee dies in service, Group Life provides financial and bereavement support to their loved ones.

- You set the core cover amount and can allow employees to increase their cover if they wish.
- We'll make a lump sum payment to the scheme trustees who then pass it on to any beneficiary(s).

[Learn more about Group Life Insurance "Open our group life insurance page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/group-life-insurance/)

### Spouse or Partner Life Insurance

This works similarly to Group Life and provides a lump sum if an employee’s spouse or partner dies.

- You choose whether this option is available and the increase steps, up to a maximum of £250,000.
- We’ll make a lump sum payment if a claim is made.

### Group Income Protection

Provides up to 80% of an employee’s salary if they can’t work due to illness or injury. They’ll also get rehabilitation support to help them back to work when they’re ready.

- You decide the cover options and payment terms. Employees can select their cover level - 50%, 66%, 75%, or 80% of salary - within the limits you set.
- You can also choose whether to offer flexibility on payment duration.

[Learn more about Group Income Protection "Open our group income protection page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/group-protection/group-income-protection-insurance/)
