## Tailoring Optimum to suit your business and your people

With Optimum, you decide what is and isn’t included. From extending the list of hospitals available for treatment to including dental and optical cover, you can tailor your cover to what your business, and employees, need.

Starting with core cover, you can choose to add more benefits, which will increase your premium. Or, if options in your business private health insurance aren’t needed anymore, you can select options to reduce your cover.

### Core cover includes:

- **Acute conditions**

Treatment for short-term illnesses or injuries that respond quickly to treatment, and lead to a full recovery.
- **Hospital charges and specialist fees**

Cover for things like out-patient consultations and treatment, as well as specialists' fees and hospital accommodation.
- **Treatment for back, neck, muscle or joint pain (musculoskeletal conditions)**

Optimum includes BacktoBetter, a third-party musculoskeletal case management service for members, to help them get better and back to work quicker.
- **Cancer cover**

Including an NHS cancer cash benefit and radiotherapy and chemotherapy – on referral from a specialist.
- **Diagnostic tests**

Such as pathology, X-rays, CT scans and physiological tests such as ECGs.

### Enhance your cover:

- Upgrade to our extended hospital list
- Move up to level 3 cancer cover
- Add our mental health pathway
- Add our neurodevelopmental pathway
- Include gender identity support and services
- Add fertility and family planning support
- Include routine and GP referred services, such as scans, physiotherapy, and consultations and tests for chronic conditions
- Add dental and optical cover

### Reduce your cover:

- Lowering the number of hospitals available for treatment
- Adding a member excess
- Reducing out-patient cover
- Going down to level 1 cancer cover
- Selecting the six week option

## Support your employees can rely on

When your employees aren't feeling their best, Optimum offers them expert support when they most need it. This includes extensive cancer care and direct access to both mental health and musculoskeletal treatment through our managed pathways.

### Cancer cover

Cancer care is included in our core cover for Optimum, and there are three levels of cover you can choose from.

That means you have the flexibility to offer the cancer cover that suits your members’ needs and your budget. While all levels cover diagnosis and treatment, higher levels can include more high-cost treatments, and more.

Whichever level you choose, employees will get:

- access to diagnosis and treatment, helping to put their minds at ease
- an experienced and dedicated cancer claims team to support them every step of the way
- a range of additional support to help them throughout their cancer journey and beyond
- unlimited access to talking therapies

See how we supported Karin, one of our customers since 2021, with [her cancer journey](https://bcove.video/4t9L3wl "Karin's cancer story video").

### Mental Health Pathway

Our Mental Health Pathway offers your employees access to talking therapy and counselling, without the need for a GP referral. When they call us, we'll put them in touch with our third-party clinical provider for assessment and treatment. Outpatient limits don't apply to Mental Health Pathway.

We offer a wide range of specialists through our network of professionals. This includes clinical psychologists, talking therapists, psychiatrists and counsellors.

Anyone over the age of 11 who’s covered on the policy can use Mental Health Pathway.

### BacktoBetter

A musculoskeletal condition – affecting back, neck, muscles or joints – can get worse quickly if not treated. When employees call our BacktoBetter service, they’ll be referred to one of our third-party clinical providers who’ll suggest the right treatment pathway for them.

If physiotherapy is recommended, we’ll pay for it when it’s needed. No need for a GP referral.

Anyone over the age of 11 who’s covered on the policy can use BacktoBetter.

## Looking to offer more to your employees?

If Optimum isn’t the right fit for your business, Optimum Referral or a Private Medical Trust could be. They can help you offer a further range of medical care for your employees.

### Optimum Referral

A healthcare product that draws upon our breadth of clinical and customer service expertise, Optimum Referral delivers quality, sustainable healthcare tailored to you and your members’ needs.

With an open referral approach, it gives us the chance to add real value to your members. We use our clinical knowledge and independent quality data to signpost them to the right treatment provider for them. And we help to keep private medical insurance sustainable by making sure your healthcare is procured effectively and efficiently.

### Private Medical Trusts

If you have 500 or more employees, you may choose to fund your corporate private healthcare with a healthcare trust. A healthcare trust can give you more control over the type of medical benefits you provide, as you can set the rules of the trust. And we can provide the day-to-day admin and management of the trust, including claims assessment and payment, so you don't have to.

Because it’s not an insurance contract, choosing a healthcare trust comes with funding benefits, like not paying Insurance Premium Tax on the claims fund. It can also provide Stop Loss Insurance to protect your company’s liability by absorbing the exposure to costs above a certain level.
