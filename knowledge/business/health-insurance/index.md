## Answering your questions

We know there’s a lot to understand about business health insurance before you can make a decision. Below, you can find answers to some of the most common questions we get about it.

## What is business health insurance?Show

Getting business health insurance is just like getting private health insurance, except instead of covering just you, it covers your employees too. They can claim on that insurance if they need to. It means your employees get access to a range of medical cover, which can vary depending on the business health insurance policy you choose.

## Why do you need business health insurance?Show

Having good health cover can not only be an enticing benefit for candidates thinking of joining you, it can also be a great boost to your business. That’s because private healthcare can help your employees take care of their health, so they’re less likely to need to take sick leave. It can also help support their return to work if they have been away.

Private medical healthcare is usually quicker and easier, too. With video consultations and lower waiting times, your people can get the help they need sooner and even get back to work quicker.

## How do you choose the right option for your business?Show

To start with, you need to think about how many employees you have and what your budget is. Your budget will help you decide a lot of key questions, including:

- how much cover you want to offer your employees
- the amount they need to pay in (usually referred to as the excess)
- whether you want to give them the option of adding their family members to their policy
- if you can add any extras, like gym memberships

## What do you need to apply?Show

If you’ve chosen your plan, get in touch to apply either online or over the phone. We’ll ask you for some details about your business, so keep any information you might need to hand. Then, we’ll work with you to shape your offer, helping you to choose what’s included and adding or removing any extras, and give you a quote for your business health insurance.
