# Contact us about Business Health Insurance

## Getting in touch with us is easy

- Talk to us to get a quote to suit the size of your business
- Find out how to make a claim on your health insurance
- Our friendly expert team are here to help you look after your people

## Get a quote

Our business health insurance could work for your business, no matter the size of your workforce.

Adding a business health insurance policy to your range of benefits will show your employees how much you value them and care about their health and wellbeing. It can also help you attract and retain staff, building resilience within your business.

Our team can help you tailor a quote to suit the needs of your business and your employees.

### Need cover for 1-99 employees?

Call **[0800 158 4055](tel:08001584055 "Call 08001584055")**

Lines are open Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm

For our joint protection, we may record and/or monitor telephone calls.

[Request a callback "Go to private business insurance call back page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/health-insurance/business-pmi/call-back/)

## Make a claim

It’s up to your employee to call us to start a claim once they have a referral from their GP.

### For schemes covering 1-99 employees

Call **[0800 158 3333](tel:%2008001583333 "Call: 08001583333")**

Monday to Friday: 8am to 6.30pm

Saturday: 8am to 1pm

Your employees can also submit a claim online through [MyAviva](https://www.direct.aviva.co.uk/MyAccount/login "go to MyAviva log in page").

For our joint protection, we may record and/or monitor telephone calls.

### For schemes covering 100 or more employees

Call **[0800 158 3344](tel:%2008001583344 "Call: 08001583344")**

Monday to Friday: 8am to 6.30pm

Saturday: 8am to 1pm

Your employees can also submit a claim online through [MyAviva](https://www.direct.aviva.co.uk/MyAccount/login "go to MyAviva log in page").

For our joint protection, we may record and/or monitor telephone calls.
