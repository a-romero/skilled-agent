# Group Personal Pension

This page describes Aviva's Group Personal Pension (GPP), a workplace pension scheme designed for small to medium-sized businesses with at least 10 employees that can be set up and managed online in minutes. It outlines the key features, benefits, investment options, costs, and how to get a quote or apply.
