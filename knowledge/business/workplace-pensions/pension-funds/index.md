# Pension Funds for Aviva workplace pensions

## Help your members build the retirement they deserve

- Well-designed default funds that aim to reduce investment risk as members get closer to retirement
- A wide range of fund choices that include sustainable and Shariah options
- Members can check and manage their pension and investment options easily online

## What is a pension fund?

A pension fund is used to help build a retirement income for members of a workplace pension scheme by investing pension contributions from members and their employers. The investments are professionally managed by experienced fund managers with the aim of growing the value of the contributions.

The pension fund options available with our workplace pensions are all managed by carefully selected global fund management groups or by our own global asset manager Aviva Investors.

Here you can explore the main pension fund options available with our workplace pension products.

The value of a pension can go down a well as up, so members could get back less than paid in.

## Our workplace pension fund options

### My Future Focus

The My Future Focus strategy aims to combine the best of both worlds by balancing risk and reward for your employees in their retirement journey. This is achieved through investment in a broad range of asset classes. My Future Focus adapts to the different stages of the pension journey and mainly invests in equities in the growth stage, before dialling down risk as members get closer to retirement.

This is the default pension fund for our [Group Personal Pension](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/group-personal-pension/) and the [Aviva Master Trust](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/aviva-master-trust/).

[Explore My Future Focus "Click to go to our My Future Focus page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/pension-funds/my-future-focus/)

### My Future

The My Future strategy changes the balance of investments through the retirement journey. In a similar way to My Future Focus, it mainly invests in equities in the growth phase of the pension and then focuses on capital preservation in the lead-up to retirement.

My Future is the default pension fund for our [Group Self-invested Personal Pension](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/group-self-invested-personal-pension/).

[Explore My Future "Click to go to our My Future Focus page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/pension-funds/my-future/)

### My Future Vision

My Future Vision is our newest default strategy, designed to give members greater access to private markets asset classes. It uses our well-established multi-fund and glidepath model to help your employees  prepare for their retirement.

My Future Vision is only available for My Money trust-based schemes.

[Explore My Future Vision "Click to go to our My Future Vision page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/pension-funds/my-future-vision/)

### Stewardship

Introduced in 1984, the Stewardship funds were the UK's first ethical funds of their kind. They’ve been managed by Aviva Investors since 2018.

The Stewardship funds exclude companies that could be deemed harmful to the world and its people. The remaining companies are assessed for investment by a review of their products and services and operations. Businesses must show that they contribute to one or more of Aviva Investors' sustainability pillars - Climate, Earth and People.

There are five funds available, including an international equity fund, two UK equity funds and a bond fund.

[Explore Stewardship "Click to go to our Stewardship page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/pension-funds/stewardship/)

### Self-select funds

These funds let your employees invest their pension in funds outside the default option, giving them more control over their savings. The funds within the self-select range are managed by leading global asset managers, and cover the main asset classes - equities, bonds and property.

We also offer a choice of funds that take environmental, social and governance (ESG) factors into consideration, as well as ethical funds.

This includes funds which have been given a Sustainability Disclosure (SDR) label by the FCA. These funds have specific environmental and/or social goals.

[Explore self-select funds "Click to go to our My Future Focus page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/pension-funds/self-select-funds/)

### Shariah-compliant investing

Shariah-compliant investing draws on the expertise of Islamic scholars and other experts to make sure a fund only invests in businesses that operate, within the principles of the Islamic faith.  The approach works in the same way as an ethical fund, screening out investments that do not meet the requirements of Shariah principles – like alcohol, pork and tobacco.

[See Shariah-compliant investing "Click here to visit our Shariah-compliant investing page"](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/workplace-pensions/pension-funds/shariah-investment-strategy/)
