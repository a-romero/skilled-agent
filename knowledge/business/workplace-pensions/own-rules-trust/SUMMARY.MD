# Own Rules Trust

This page describes Aviva's Own Rules Trust services, a workplace pension solution that allows companies to retain their own board of trustees while outsourcing scheme management and administration to Aviva. It covers the benefits, features, investment options, and support provided for setting up and managing trust-based pension schemes.
