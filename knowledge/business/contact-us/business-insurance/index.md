# Contact us about business insurance

## How can we help?

To see how to contact us, please select the insurance you're enquiring about, below.

**If you bought your business insurance from a broker, please contact them directly for any claims, changes to your cover or if you are struggling to pay for your insurance.**

For your protection and ours, calls to and from Aviva may be recorded and/or monitored. Calls to 0800 numbers from UK landlines and mobiles are free of charge. Calls from outside the UK may be charged at international rates.. The cost of calls to 03 prefixed numbers are charged at national call rates (charges may vary dependent on your network provider) and are usually included in inclusive minute plans from landlines and mobiles.
