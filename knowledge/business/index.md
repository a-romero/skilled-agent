## How we’re pushing towards a brighter future for all of us

Thinking about the world we live and operate in over the longer term is driving us to create a more sustainable business – and a more resilient UK. We do this through action in three main areas.

![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/Images/business/workplace-pension/business-homepage/business-homepage-pod-social-action-1620x910px.$16x9-mobile-6cols$.jpg?$16x9-mobile-6cols$)

### Social action ![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/icons/business/user-icons/people-icon-black.svg)

- We’ve contributed over £7 million to our partner Citizens Advice to help people become more financially resilient.
- We’ve contributed more than £17 million to over 9,000 community-based causes since 2015.
- We’ve invested billions in UK infrastructure, including urban regeneration, social housing, renewable energy, and EV recharging networks.

![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/Images/business/workplace-pension/business-homepage/business-homepage-pod-climate-action-1620x910px.$16x9-mobile-6cols$.jpg?$16x9-mobile-6cols$)

### Climate action ![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/icons/business/user-icons/thermometer-gain-icon-black.svg)

- We’re pursuing our ambition to be Net Zero by 2040 – and we are working with our supply chain to share this ambition.
- We use 100% renewable electricity for all our offices across core markets.
- We’ve partnered with leading conservation charities on nature-based solutions, like restoring wetlands, woodlands and peatlands.

![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/Images/business/workplace-pension/business-homepage/business-homepage-pod-climate-sustainability1620x910px.$16x9-mobile-6cols$.jpg?$16x9-mobile-6cols$)

### Embedding sustainability ![](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-business/gb/icons/business/user-icons/sync-icon-black.svg)

- We aim to act as a trusted sustainability leader. Our actions focus on providing purposeful proposition choice, being the employer of choice, and protecting human rights while maintaining good governance.
- We’ve invested £8.7 billion pounds in sustainable assets since the end of 2019.
- In 2024, we launched a [Sustainability Resources and Reporting hub](https://www.aviva.com/sustainability/resources-and-reporting-hub/ "Go to Go to aviva.com sustainability, resources and reporting page") to share our commitment to being a responsible and transparent organisation.

While we are working towards our sustainability ambition, we acknowledge that when it comes to decarbonising the economy in which we operate and invest, Aviva is one part of a far larger global ecosystem. There are limits to our ability to influence other organisations and governments. Nevertheless, we remain focused on the task and are committed to playing our part in the collective effort to enable the global transition.

[Read more on sustainability](https://www.aviva.com/sustainability/)
