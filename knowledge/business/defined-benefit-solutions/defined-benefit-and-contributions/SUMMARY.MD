# Defined Benefit And Contributions

This page describes Aviva's Defined Benefit and Contribution (DB&C) solutions for pension scheme Trustees, offering flexible options to secure members' benefits that combine both DB and DC elements. It outlines three main solutions: Aviva Master Trust, External DC provider, and Protected benefits options.
