## Sole trader or company.  What’s the difference?

Pick the option that fits you to make sure you’re getting the right kind of business vehicle insurance.

You’re the owner (or owners) but there’s no legal difference between you and your business. So you registered the vehicle in your name. This means you’ll need sole trader cover.

## Why do I need Business Vehicle Insurance?

You'll need business vehicle and van insurance if you use your van for any kind of work reasons, like driving to and from a job site or using it to help run your small business.

The van can be registered in your company's name or, if you're a sole trader, registered in your own name.

### Comprehensive insurance

Whats covered

- **Repair guarantee** Following a claim we guarantee the quality of repairs made by our approved repairers for as long as you own your van, even if you change insurers.

Parts are covered for the length of the guarantee given by the part manufacturer or supplier. Wear and tear is not covered. You must keep your vehicle roadworthy.
- **Up to £500 tool cover** The tools of your trade that you keep in your business vehicle or van are covered up to the value of £500 with our comprehensive insurance. We won't cover loss or damage arising from theft not involving forcible entry to your van.
- **Passenger cover for your colleagues** Not all commercial vehicle policies will allow you to carry passengers, but ours does. So you’re still covered if you give a colleague a lift to the office or a site.

- **New vehicle replacement** When you've got our comprehensive cover, we'll supply replacement vehicles so your business stay on the road.
- **Straightforward claims** With our straightforward online claims process and 24/7 claims helpline for new claims only.

What’s not covered

Like most Business Vehicle Insurance policies and Van Insurance policies, we can’t cover you if your car or van is being used for purposes that aren’t under the description of use, or your vehicle is being driven by a driver your policy doesn’t cover. [full list of exclusions (PDF 256KB)](https://www.aviva.co.uk/static/library/pdfs/motor/multi-car/NMDMG10249.pdf "full list of exclusions")

- **Business goods** Any goods or samples you carry for your trade or business
- **Tyres** Any punctures, cuts or bursts – including those caused by braking
- **Resale value** We can’t cover you for any loss in value of your van after repair

What's optional

- **Foreign use cover** Only available with our comprehensive cover.
- **Protected no claim discount**

You won't lose your no claim discount as a direct result of a single claim for the next year
- **Motor legal** This gives an expert legal helping hand and costs of up to £100,000 to recover compensation after a vehicle accident, when you did nothing wrong. It covers you, your named drivers and any passengers. You’ll only get this support if it’s likely you’ll win your claim against the person responsible. It also provides up to £10,000 for lawyers' costs to defend you against prosecution for a motoring offence.
- **Courtesy vehicle Cover** We’ll keep you on the road with a courtesy vehicle if yours is damaged, written off, or stolen. As part of our comprehensive cover, you can choose either a car or van as an optional extra. Ideal for drivers who rely on larger vehicles for work or transport. Exclusions and limitations apply.
- **UK Breakdown cover** Roadside assistance, Recovery, At Home and Onward Travel as an add-on to your business vehicle insurance. Not available if you live in Northern Ireland. [Breakdown cover terms and conditions (PDF 223 KB)](https://static.aviva.io/content/dam/aviva-public/gb/pdfs/personal/insurance/motor/car/breakdown-cover-terms-and-conditions.pdf).

### Third party, fire and theft

Whats covered

- **Damage to your van** Protection against loss or damage to your van due to fire, theft, or attempted theft
- **Van recovery** Removal of your van if damaged by fire, theft, or attempted theft
- **Your liability** Cover if you injure someone or damage their vehicle or property in a motor accident

What’s not covered

Like most Business Vehicle Insurance policies and Van Insurance policies, we can’t cover you if your car or van is being used for purposes that aren’t under the description of use, or your vehicle is being driven by a driver your policy doesn’t cover.

- **Glass replacement** Any repair or replacement to glass in your windscreen, sunroof, or windows
- **Medical expenses** Any medical expenses if you’ve been injured in an accident
- **Personal belongings** Damage to any of your personal belongings
- **Replacement locks** Locks, transmitters, and immobilisers if your keys are lost or stolen
- **Tool cover** Any replacement tools if your van is broken into
- **New van replacement**

Not included with this type of cover

## Got a question?

We answer some of the most frequently asked questions about Aviva Signature Business Vehicle Insurance.

#### How do I change my details on my motor insurance? Show

Please call us on [0345 030 8870](tel:03450308870 "0345 030 8870") [Footnote \[2\]](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/insurance/business-vehicle-insurance/#footnote-2) to change your address, registration number or payment details with your information to hand and we’ll update your records for you.

#### Do you charge an admin fee to make a change? Show

No we do not charge an admin fee.

#### How can I get proof of/transfer my no claim discount? Show

If your policy’s due for renewal, the renewal schedule or letter should count as evidence of your no claim discount (NCD) for your next insurer.

If you cancel at any other point, and have been insured with us for more than a year, we’ll automatically include proof of your NCD with your cancellation letter. If you’d prefer this via email, you can request this when you cancel.

If you’ve been with us for less than a year, we’ll send you a separate letter confirming how long you have been claim free for.

Please call us on [0345 030 8870](tel:03450308870 "0345 030 8870") [Footnote \[2\]](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/insurance/business-vehicle-insurance/#footnote-2) if you’ve already cancelled your policy and no longer have your documents.

You can choose to transfer your personal NCD to your business vehicle insurance policy. If your business trades as a registered company, you cannot transfer it back to your personal policy, the transfer will be permanent and your NCD will belong to your business. However, if you operate as a sole trader, you can transfer it back to your domestic vehicle policy.

#### Am I entitled to driving other cars (DOC) cover? Show

DOC cover only applies to the vehicle policy holder of sole trader policy and that there is no DOC cover on a company policy.

- Have comprehensive cover
- Are aged 25 or above (at the start date or renewal of the policy).

Applicable drivers can drive any other cars, third party only, with the permission of the owner that are not owned by or registered to them.

For cover limits, exclusions and further information please review your policy documents.

#### How do I add or remove a driver or change my vehicle? Show

Please call us on [0345 030 8870](tel:03450308870 "0345 030 8870") [Footnote \[2\]](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/insurance/business-vehicle-insurance/#footnote-2). If you're adding a driver, we'll need to know the driver's details including information about any claims or motoring convictions before we can give you a quote. If you’re changing your vehicle, there may be a change to your premium - this will depend on your new vehicle details.

#### Temporary changes and renewals Show

Please call us on [0345 030 8870](tel:03450308870 "0345 030 8870") [Footnote \[2\]](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/insurance/business-vehicle-insurance/#footnote-2) if you want to make a temporary change or you’re approaching your renewal date.

#### What if I've got more than one vehicle? Show

If you’ve got more than one business vehicle registered at the same address, you can get up to 10% off your business vehicle insurance with our Aviva Signature Multi Car policy. [Footnote \[3\]](https://www.aviva.co.uk/business/insurance/business-vehicle-insurance/#footnote-3) Discount doesn't apply to optional extras.
