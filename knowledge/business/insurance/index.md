## An update on Aviva’s business insurance offering

After a recent review of the best way to make sure our customers have the cover they need, we’ve decided to no longer offer business insurance directly to new small businesses customers (with the exception of Business Vehicle and Landlord Insurance). Instead, we're linking you to [PIB Insurance Brokers](https://www.pib-insurance.com/aviva "Visit PIB's website to obtain a quote for your businesses insurance needs"), a broker with extensive experience in the business insurance market, with whom we have a long-standing relationship.

We will continue to support and cover existing customers until their next renewal.  We will notify existing customers 90 days before renewal to help with alternative cover arrangements.

With individual business needs ever changing, we believe directing you to a broker with the expertise and knowledge to recommend the right cover is the best way we can support small businesses.
